Instructions
========================

Buttons are on a 8x8 grid. They are can be tiled or used as 9patch.

Buttons have:

A cross button to make curves in buttons like a return key. 
And the 4 variations with are a subset of the cross buttons:
* Big Square button
* Horizontal button
* Vertical button
* Small Square button

See examples on how to build buttons.

They come in the same colours as the windows.
